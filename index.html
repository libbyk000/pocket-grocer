pocket grocer

Track your groceries, find recipes, and eliminate food waste!

track your groceries

After your trip to the store, log in to our website to record your groceries in our digital pantry. You can even create a shared pantry with your friends!

find recipes

Running low on ingredients and unsure what to make? Our website will warn you when foods are about to expire and suggest healthy recipes based on the ingredients you have left.

minimize waste

With our food waste dashboard, you'll be able to pinpoint which foods you end up throwing away, and adjust your shopping list for future trips to save time and money.
